About Us

LAP (learn And Play) we decided to create a new method & new way to make the educational process more fun, easier & entertaining.
We are using the student senses as seeing & listening.
By creating games and curriculum simulations using AR (Augment Reality ) we are offering the student the ability to use their creativity & imagination.
The students will be able to see their lessons as 3D Models which will make the knowledge they receive more understandable and easier.
This feature will be provided for all the school subjects such as Geography, Biology, etc. In this way, we will be able to facilitate the educational process for all the students especially those who have faced learning difficulties We are targeting all students and also special needs students, such as those who have ADHD.
